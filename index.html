THE LUNGS OF OUR PLANET

STOP EXPLOITATION OF THE AMAZON RAINFOREST

amazon forever

A global nonprofit organisation that creates awareness around the issues of deforestation in the Amazon Rainforest.

Amazon Forever are working to conserve the biodiversity of the Amazon basin through the development of new scientific understanding, sustainable resource management and rational land-use policy.

FUTURE

17.7 Million hectares have been destroyed since 2001. WWF estimate the Amazon will loose a further 48 million hectares by 2030.

BIODIVERSITY

The Amazon is home to at least 10% of all known species on Earth.

It also houses endangered flora and fauna.

DEFORESTATION

More than 20% of the Amazon rainforest is already gone, and much more is severely threatened as the destruction continues.

A CONSERVATION CHARITY THAT WORKS

Protection requires swift action, and we implement scientifically-based conservation plans that are not only timely, but also resilient and sustainable. With your support, we work tirelessly to permanently protect rainforests and other vital habitat throughout the world.

THIS IS WHAT CHANGE LOOKS LIKE

Last year we saved 1.09 million acres of rainforest, around 1 acre every 16 seconds. We launched our new Fellows and Guardians Programme to support our on the ground conservationists and we've grown from a small non-profit to an internationally recognised organisation allowing us to expand the scoop of our conservation.
